Privacy Policy

EazzySpeach · Effective date: January 2026

EazzySpeach ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

EazzySpeach does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The app processes only the text content provided by the user for AI-powered translation. This text is sent to a trusted third-party service solely to perform the requested translation.

Data Usage

  • Translated text is not stored on our servers
  • Text data is used only to provide the translation feature
  • No personal data is sold or shared

Advertising

EazzySpeach displays ads using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, frequency capping, and performance measurement, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

If required by law or platform policy, EazzySpeach will request user permission before enabling ad tracking, in compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API — AI-powered text translation
  • Google AdMob — advertising services

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

EazzySpeach does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
