Privacy Policy

EazzySpeach respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

EazzySpeach does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The app processes only the text content provided by the user solely for AI-powered translation purposes. This text is sent to trusted third-party services only to perform the requested translation.

Data Usage

  • Translated text is not stored on our servers
  • Text data is used only to provide the translation feature
  • No personal data is sold or shared

Advertising

EazzySpeach displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, frequency capping, and performance measurement, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

If required by law or platform policy, EazzySpeach will request user permission before enabling ad tracking, in compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API — Transcription and AI-powered text translation
  • Microsoft Translator (Azure Cognitive Services) — text translation
  • Google AdMob — advertising services

These services operate under their own respective privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

EazzySpeach does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

Какие данные мы собираем

EazzySpeach не собирает, не хранит и не передаёт персональные данные пользователей.

Приложение обрабатывает только текст, предоставленный пользователем, исключительно с целью AI-перевода. Этот текст передаётся надёжным сторонним сервисам только для выполнения запрошенного перевода.
